# Switch sound samples

Recordings of real mechanical switches, taken from [tplai/kbsim](https://github.com/tplai/kbsim)
(MIT licensed). Four packs are bundled: `holypanda`, `boxnavy`, `cream`, `mxbrown`.

Each pack has:

- `press/GENERIC_R0…R4.mp3` — one sample per keyboard row, so rows sound slightly different
- `press/SPACE.mp3`, `press/ENTER.mp3`, `press/BACKSPACE.mp3` — stabilised keys
- `release/*.mp3` — the up-stroke
